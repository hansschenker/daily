What is apob test
------------------
This test measures the amount of a certain type of protein on the surface of cholesterol 
called apolipoprotein B-100 (ApoB) in your blood. 

ApoB is the main protein found in the low-density lipoproteins (LDL). 
LDL cholesterol is known as "bad" cholesterol because high levels 
of it can damage your heart and arteries.
